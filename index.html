Run Frost Run

Run Frost Run is endless runner game, although it resembles the 2D platformer, its unique gameplay, however, does not fit to neither category. The difference is, that the main character is not directly controlled by the player. The progress of the main character Frost the penguin is achieved by disposing the barriers and obstacles from his way.

  • Easy gameplay using touchscreen.
Download from Google Play or AppGallery .

Garden Gnome

Garden Gnome is not an usual arcade game - It is the mix of shoot'em up and defence game. The game shares some ideas with the Nintendo's Donkey Kong 3 arcade and PSSST ZX Spectrum game. In our game, the main character equipped with the bug spray is Garden Gnome. His goal is to protect various types of flowers against the hungry insect. A level is completed by continually watering the flowers in order to make them bloom.

  • The game supports bluetooth controller.
  • Suitable for the Android TV. Fully controllable by the remote.
Screenshots available at Google Play.
